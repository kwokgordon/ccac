“Discovery of one’s God-given Servant Profile”

Teachers: David Fong, Sam Mah

The main goal of this class is to help participants to identify their unique servant profiles. In so doing, this class addresses and provides answers to a couple fundamental questions such as “why do we serve? and how are we to serve?” We serve to glory God and to edify others, which are tied to the ultimate purpose of life for all Christians: worship and mission. The servant profile in essence identifies our spiritual gifts, personal style, and ministry passion. Spiritual gifts indicate what you do best, and you will be competent when you serve using them. Personal style indicates how you best serve, and you will be confident when you serve in line with it. Ministry passion indicates where you best serve, and you will be motivated when you serve in it. Hence, we must serve according to our unique servant profile so that we will be fruitful and fulfilled.
