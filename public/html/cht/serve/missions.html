Missions

The Missions Ministry works to proclaim and to promote the gospel to people who haven't accepted Jesus Christ in their life yet. We attend mission conferences, embark on short term missions (locally, regionally and internationally), invite C&MA missionaries for visit & sharing, as well as support and partnership with local organizations with a Missions focus.

 

To learn about upcoming Missions opportunities talk to a Pastor or emailenglish@calgarychinesealliance.org.
