Children's Ministry

Age range: toddlers to elementary age children (1.5 years to 12 years).

The Children's Ministry believes family is fundamental in a child’s spiritual life and their early decision to follow Jesus Christ. We are committed to reaching out, supporting and encouraging families and children to know Christ’s love and presence in their lives. We long for children to feel valued and loved as children of God, be encouraged and equipped through the Holy Spirit in their early walk with the Lord, and be a part of a supportive Christian community.

The Children's Ministry has Sunday worship for pre-schoolers, kindergarteners, and elementary age children. We also offer Sunday School for the same age group of children. There is also a toddlers program for children ages 1.5 years to 3 years.

 

Awana

Awana®  is a ministry that provides a time for kids to learn about Jesus Christ and build a biblical foundation for faith. We come together every first and third Saturday evening of the month to play games, learn Bible stories and Bible verses, experience Christian values and grow together in community. All are welcome, registration required.
