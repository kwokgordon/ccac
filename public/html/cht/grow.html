Grow

We are all the body of Christ and together we can help you grow in your walk with Christ.

Sunday Worship - Our weekend service offered in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. Click here for service times.

Sunday School - We offer classes to help you learn about building a relationship with God. Sunday School is after the English Worship service.

Children's Ministry - Your children can learn about God through fun, interactive Bible lessons. Children from nursery age to grade 6 can join our classes offered during Sunday Worship.

Youth Ministry - Jr. & Sr. High students are encouraged to attend the Sunday Worship service, and are invited to join our Youth Sunday School after the English Worship Service.

Library - CCAC has an extensive collection of books in English, Simplified and Traditional Chinese.
