Married Couples (MC)

We are dedicated to growing and strengthening our marriage in a community. By listening, sharing, caring and learning, we strive to live out a Christ-centered marriage. We serve young adult couples that are married or engaged. We meet in small groups that consist of approximately 4-6 couples, depending on the group. The groups meet together to support each other through the early marriage years in order to develop relationships with a strong foundation in Christ. Our group currently has couples which range in age from the early 20’s to late 30’s, with some families who have young children.
