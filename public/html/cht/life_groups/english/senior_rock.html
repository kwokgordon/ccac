Senior Rock

Senior Rock is held Friday nights for Senior high school students (Grades 10-12). It is a Friday night fellowship for our senior high kids, its a safe place so they can come together as a family to have fun, fellowship, and connect with others that they might not have had the opportunity to before. Our vision is to see these high school kids develope deeper relationships, and interactions with each other, the counselors, and most importantly with Christ as their intimate Savior.
