Jericho

The Jericho Fellowship is an adult English fellowship that strives to be open and inclusive to everyone while intentionally focusing on spurring one another to love God, and others, and to serve the world. By sharing our faith, experiences, lessons-learned during our life journeys, we encourage and support each other; being aware that we are models to younger generations. We express our love and obedience to Jesus our Lord and Saviour by adopting a servant’s heart and attitude, by being good stewards with our gifts and talents, and by reaching out to those that are seeking what we have: a personal relationship with a living God.

We meet twice a month: Bible study the second Thursday of the month and a large group fellowship the third Saturday of the month. We hold Bible studies at people’s homes on a rotating basis, and the group fellowship meets in the banquet room in the basement of our Church.
