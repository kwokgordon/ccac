BASIC

Brothers And Sisters In Christ (BASIC) is a ministry/fellowship that connects primarily with college students and young adults in their years following high school, generally meeting on Friday evenings with a mixture of small group and large group events. We seek to provide a safe and fun place for this young adult community to go deeper with God and others, explore their questions of life, be real and find encouragement, support and friendship through building trusting and meaningful relationships with one another. Together, we are learning to love God and one another, grow in His Word, be led by the Spirit and understand our role in God's grand plan and how to live as followers of Jesus, that our God may be glorified through our lives in witness to our family, friends and community.
