Junior Rock

Junior Rock is made up of a group of youth (Grades 7-9). The focus of Junior Rock is to start and develop strong Christian relationships between brothers and sisters in Christ that will last a lifetime. We hope to achieve this through fun individual and group events that are intended to allow the youth to build friendships based on everyday interests. These friendships are further nurtured in small group discussions and other fellowship events, which are aimed to tackle everyday issues and problems with a biblical and Christ-like perspective. The Junior Rock counselors are thrilled to share their own experiences and just hang out with the all the youth. So come out and check out what we’re all about!!!
