Career

CLAY is focused on young adults who have completed school or entering the workforce. By listening, sharing, caring, and learning, we strive to live out a Christ-centered marriage.

Our vision is to help young adults be and make mature disciples of Jesus Christ through authentic community.

CLAY learns, grows and outreaches together mainly through small groups. There are several small groups that meet regularly that explore how to be and make disciples of Jesus in various ways. There are also retreats and special events. CLAY is a part of the life of CCAC in many ways. CLAY will also look to be involved in contributing to the needs of the neighbourhoods surrounding CCAC, which may involve project-based collaborations with non-profit organizations and/or building lasting relationships with those around us.

For more info contact clay@calgarychinesealliance.org
