Serve

The Church exists to equip everyone for works of ministry (Ephesians 4). As we grow in our relationship with Jesus, our response to the Gospel is to serve because of the grace Jesus has given us and the mission He calls us to be a part of in building the church and reaching the lost. There are a variety of ways to serve at Calgary Chinese Alliance Church, as we are all gifted in unique ways.

Serve in:

Missions

Worship Arts

Technical Arts

Ushering & Hospitality

 

To learn about serving opportunities or if you would like to serve at Calgary Chinese Alliance Church talk to a Pastor, check the bulletin, or contact English@calgarychinesealliance.org.
