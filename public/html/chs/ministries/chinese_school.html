School Mission

The school is one of the ministries of the Calgary Chinese Alliance Church. A non-profit heritage language school started in 1980 with the visions of teaching Chinese language and culture to the community and helping students find a meaningful life and their purpose of life.

 

Courses

 

The school offers Cantonese classes from Grade 1 to 12 and Mandarin classes from Grade 1 to 9. We also have a Cantonese preschool class for children under 4 in preparation to Grade 1. All Grade 10 to 12 curriculums are approved by Alberta Education and students will receive 5 credits for each of the grade upon completion of the requirements.

Admission requirement and fees

 

A. Admission requirements

  1. Entrance examination set by curriculum leader/department head/principal
  2. G1 student MUST be 4 years of age on or before the last day of February in the current school year, e.g. for 14/15 school year, student must be 4 on or before February 28, 2014.
  3. Payment in full for the fees described in the section B.
