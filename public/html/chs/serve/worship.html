Worship Arts

Worship can come through many different forms and is used to express your love and passion for God.  We invite you to get involved in your area of interest and join our Worship Ministry. A team meets once a week to practice and Sunday morning services to rehearse and worship together with the CCAC congregation. You don’t need to be musically gifted to join the Worship Ministry, just a heart and passion to serve our God!

 

To get involved talk to a Pastor or email english@calgarychinesealliance.org.
