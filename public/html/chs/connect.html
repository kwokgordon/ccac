There are many English fellowships at Calgary Chinese Alliance Church that people can be involved with to learn about God, discuss your thoughts about Christ, and meet other people to take the spiritual journey with.  In the English congregation, we have several fellowships aimed to reach out and help you grow in Christ.

  • Senior Rock - Grades 10 - 12
  • BASIC - University
  • CLAY - Young Adult & Career
  • MC - Married Couples
  • Jericho - Adult
  • ESL - Open to everyone
  • Sports - Open to everyone
